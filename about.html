About Me

My name is Kate Ancheta and I am a photography enthusiast. I love capturing special moments and being able to share them with families and their loved ones. I have been taking photos all my life and started professionally in 2016.

The name 3 Pearls Photography is inspired by my three pearls, Leo, Sophia and Charlotte, seen in our family photo. Taking photos of these three over the years is really what inspired me to become a photographer as I have so much fun capturing their silliness.

I mainly specialize in taking family and newborn photos, but I also have some experience with wedding photography and hope to do more of that in the future.

If you would like to know anything else about me or would like to set up a photo shoot, please use the contact page to get ahold of me.
